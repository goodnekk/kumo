ABOUT THE LANGUAGE

Kumo is a language designed to make things happen. Verbs are best for expressing actions, that is why in kumo allmost everything is a function. Functions can be passed around as arguments:

a = 10

subtract = {
  a=a-1
  say(a)
}

while(a>0, subtract)
